Interpreting Results

The results includes four pieces of information, parts of which will be useful depending your research question

  • Overall “fit” between the laws and the ecosystem of interest

    • If your ecosystem model is based on presence/absence data (zeroes and ones), use Jaccard’s Coefficient

    • If you ecosystem model is based on different strengths of relationships between ecosystem elements, use the QAP (Quadratic Assignment Procedure) for interpretation

  • Overall “misfit” based on ratio of gaps to modeled ecosystem links

  • Specific gaps, ecosystem linkages that are not explicitly accounted for in law or regulation for a particular jurisdiction

  • Identification of laws or regulations in which two ecosystem components co-occur

  • What laws and/or regulations in which ecosystem components co-occur (and viewable text of the sections)

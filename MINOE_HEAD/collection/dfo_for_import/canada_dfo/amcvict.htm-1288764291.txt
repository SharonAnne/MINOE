22 ((11)) No person is permitted to construct works or use or develop lands within,
on, or affecting the Victoria Diamond Jubilee Marsh marshland sections in any
way that is not conducive to and in conformity with sound agricultural purposes
and practices.
((22)) For the purposes of subsection (1), uses and activities deemed to be
conducive to and in conformity with sound agricultural purposes and practices
include the following:
((aa)) the tilling of soil;
((bb)) the planting and harvesting of crops;
((cc)) the growing of grains and forage crops for livestock feed;
((dd)) the growing of grass and legume crops;
((ee)) the growing of food crops for human consumption;
((ff)) the spreading of manure;
((gg)) the spreading of commercial (chemical) and natural fertilizer;
((hh)) the spreading of limestone;
((ii)) pest and weed control;
((jj)) pre-existing, non-conforming uses, only where and as they presently exist
including, but not limited to, hunting with restricted access by permission.
((33)) For the purposes of subsection (1), works, uses or developments that are
not permitted, include the following:
((aa)) land-fill sites or dumps of any kind;
((bb)) recreational activities of any kind;
((cc)) residential or commercial structures;
((dd)) drilled wells;
((ee)) sewage treatment plants or lagoons;
((ff)) industrial structures or uses;
((gg)) ponds for non-agricultural uses;
((hh)) top soil and sod removal.

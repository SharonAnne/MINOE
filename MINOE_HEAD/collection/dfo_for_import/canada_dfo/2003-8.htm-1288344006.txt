Under sectionÂ 35 of the Protected Natural Areas Act, the Lieutenant-Governor
in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister, makes the following
Regulation:
2004, c.20, s.50

1This Regulation may be cited as the Establishment of Protected Natural Areas
Regulation - Protected Natural Areas Act.

2(1)The following areas are established as protected natural areas:
(a)each area shown as a protected natural area on the plans attached as
Schedules I-1 to I-4; and
(b)each area shown as a protected natural area on the plans attached as
Schedules II-1 to II-57.
2(2)The areas described in paragraphÂ (1)(a) are established as Class I
Protected Natural Areas and the areas described in paragraphÂ (1)(b) are
established as Class II Protected Natural Areas.
